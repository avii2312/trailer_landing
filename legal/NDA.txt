3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECIPIENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Recipient agrees to:

3.1  Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence.

3.2  Not disclose any Confidential Information to any third party without
     prior written consent from the Company.

3.3  Not use Confidential Information for any purpose other than the Purpose
     stated in Section 1.

3.4  Not copy, reproduce, reverse engineer, decompile, or create derivative
     works based on the Confidential Information.

3.5  Limit access to Confidential Information to only those individuals who
     need to know for the stated Purpose and who are bound by similar

3.6  Notify the Company immediately upon discovery of any unauthorized use
     or disclosure of Confidential Information.

3.7  Not directly or indirectly create, develop, or assist in creating a
     competing product, service, or platform that is substantially similar
     to Seekho or uses the Confidential Information.

4. EXCLUSIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Confidential Information does NOT include information that:

a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient.

c) Is independently developed by the Recipient without use of or reference
   to the Confidential Information.
d) Is disclosed with the prior written approval of the Company.
e) Is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the Recipient
   gives the Company prompt written notice.
