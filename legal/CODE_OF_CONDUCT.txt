================================================================================
                         OUR PLEDGE
================================================================================

Seekho is a peer-to-peer skill exchange platform built by students, for
students. We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, safe, and
harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of gender, gender
identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size,
race, ethnicity, age, religion, caste, nationality, or skill level.

This Code of Conduct applies to ALL Seekho spaces, including but not limited
to: the Seekho platform, college community chapters, in-person events,
online sessions, social media groups, and any communication channels
associated with Seekho.

================================================================================
                     OUR STANDARDS
================================================================================

------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXPECTED BEHAVIOR (The Good Stuff)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. BE RESPECTFUL & KIND
   - Treat everyone with dignity and respect.
   - Use welcoming and inclusive language.
   - Be considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences.

2. BE HONEST & AUTHENTIC
   - Represent your skills accurately. Don't claim expertise you don't have.
   - Use your real identity. No fake profiles or impersonation.
   - Give genuine, constructive feedback in reviews.

3. BE A GOOD LEARNING PARTNER
   - Show up on time for scheduled sessions.
   - Come prepared and be an active participant.
   - Cancel sessions with reasonable notice (at least 2 hours).
   - Be patient — everyone learns at different speeds.

4. PROTECT PRIVACY
   - Don't share personal information of other users without consent.
   - Don't screenshot or record sessions without explicit permission.
   - Respect the confidentiality of conversations.

5. CONTRIBUTE TO THE COMMUNITY
   - Help newcomers feel welcome.
   - Share knowledge freely and generously.
   - Report violations when you see them.
   - Support your campus ambassador and community events.

6. GIVE CREDIT
   - Acknowledge when you've learned something from someone.
   - Don't pass off someone else's work or ideas as your own.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
UNACCEPTABLE BEHAVIOR (Zero Tolerance)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. HARASSMENT
   - Sexual harassment, unwelcome advances, or inappropriate comments.
   - Stalking, following, or intimidating behavior (online or offline).
   - Threats of violence or violent language directed at any person.
   - Deliberate intimidation or bullying.

2. DISCRIMINATION
   - Discriminatory jokes, language, or behavior based on any protected
     characteristic (gender, caste, religion, etc.).
   - Exclusion or marginalization of individuals.
   - Casteist, sexist, racist, homophobic, or ableist remarks.

3. MISUSE OF PLATFORM
   - Using Seekho for dating, solicitation, or any non-educational purpose.
   - Spamming, scamming, or phishing other users.
   - Creating fake accounts or manipulating reviews/aura scores.
   - Selling, promoting, or advertising products/services without approval.
   - Using the platform to collect personal data for unauthorized purposes.

4. ACADEMIC DISHONESTY
   - Using Seekho to complete assignments, exams, or coursework on behalf
     of another student (cheating-as-a-service).
   - Plagiarism or helping others plagiarize.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY VIOLATIONS
   - Sharing copyrighted materials without permission.
   - Copying or replicating Seekho's platform, features, or branding.
   - Reverse engineering or scraping the platform.

6. HARMFUL CONTENT
   - Sharing illegal, violent, or explicit content.
   - Promoting substance abuse, self-harm, or dangerous activities.
   - Spreading misinformation or propaganda.

7. DISRUPTION
   - Deliberately disrupting sessions, events, or community activities.
   - Trolling, flame wars, or sustained negativity.
   - Abusing the reporting system with false or malicious reports.

================================================================================
                     COMMUNITY CHAPTERS
================================================================================

Each college community chapter must:

1. Follow this Code of Conduct in all activities.
2. Have at least one designated moderator/ambassador.
3. Not use the Seekho brand for unauthorized purposes.
4. Not collect money on behalf of Seekho without written authorization.
5. Not organize events that contradict Seekho's values.
6. Report any incidents to the Seekho core team within 24 hours.

================================================================================
                     EVENT GUIDELINES
================================================================================

All Seekho-affiliated events (online and offline) must:

1. Be inclusive and open to all eligible participants.
2. Have a designated Code of Conduct contact person.
3. Provide a safe way for attendees to report violations.
4. Not serve alcohol or promote substance use.
5. Follow venue safety guidelines and local regulations.
6. Have at least 2 organizers present at all times.
7. Not be used for political campaigning or religious proselytizing.

================================================================================
                REPORTING & ENFORCEMENT
================================================================================

------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO REPORT
------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you experience or witness a violation, you can report it through:

1. IN-APP: Use the "Report" button on any profile, session, or message.
2. EMAIL: conduct@seekho.app (or your actual email)
3. AMBASSADOR: Contact your college's campus ambassador directly.
4. ANONYMOUS: Use the anonymous report form at seekho.app/report

All reports are treated CONFIDENTIALLY. We will never reveal a reporter's
identity without their explicit consent, except where required by law.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT HAPPENS AFTER A REPORT
------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. ACKNOWLEDGMENT: We acknowledge receipt within 24 hours.
2. INVESTIGATION: Our team reviews the report, gathers evidence, and
   contacts relevant parties within 48-72 hours.
3. DECISION: A resolution is determined based on the severity.
4. COMMUNICATION: Both parties are informed of the outcome.
5. APPEAL: The accused party may appeal within 7 days.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
ENFORCEMENT ACTIONS
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Violations will result in consequences proportional to the severity:

LEVEL 1 - WARNING
- First-time minor violations.
- Written warning via email/in-app notification.
- Violation recorded on account.

LEVEL 2 - TEMPORARY RESTRICTION
- Repeated minor violations or moderate violations.
- Temporary suspension of account (7-30 days).
- Removal from community events during suspension.
- Loss of aura points.

LEVEL 3 - PERMANENT BAN
- Severe violations or repeated moderate violations.
- Permanent removal from the platform and all communities.
- Ban from all Seekho events.
- Notification to campus ambassador and college authorities
  (if applicable and appropriate).

LEVEL 4 - LEGAL ACTION
- Illegal activities, threats of violence, or data theft.
- Permanent ban plus referral to law enforcement.
- Legal proceedings as deemed necessary.

NOTE: Seekho reserves the right to take any enforcement action at any
level, regardless of prior warnings, depending on the severity of the
violation.

================================================================================
                     AMBASSADOR RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

Campus Ambassadors have an elevated responsibility to:

1. Lead by example and uphold this Code of Conduct at all times.
2. Actively moderate their college's community.
3. Respond to reports and escalate to the core team when needed.
4. Not abuse their moderator privileges.
5. Attend mandatory training on handling violations.

Ambassadors who violate this Code of Conduct will face stricter
consequences, including immediate removal from the ambassador program.

================================================================================
                     CHANGES & UPDATES
================================================================================

Seekho reserves the right to update this Code of Conduct at any time.
Significant changes will be communicated to all users via email and
in-app notification. Continued use of the platform after changes
constitutes acceptance of the updated Code of Conduct.
