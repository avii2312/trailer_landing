================================================================================
                    1. APPOINTMENT
================================================================================

1.1  Seekho hereby appoints the Ambassador as a Campus Ambassador for
     the college/university mentioned above.

1.2  This is a VOLUNTARY, NON-EMPLOYMENT role. The Ambassador is NOT an

1.3  The Ambassador shall not represent themselves as an employee or
     authorized agent of Seekho for any legal or financial purposes.

1.4  This appointment is personal and non-transferable.

================================================================================
                  2. TERM & DURATION
================================================================================

2.1  This Agreement begins on _______________ and continues for a period
     of ___ months (the "Term").

2.2  The Agreement may be renewed by mutual written consent.

2.3  Either party may terminate this Agreement with 15 days written notice.

2.4  Seekho may terminate immediately without notice if the Ambassador
     violates this Agreement, the Code of Conduct, or brings disrepute
     to Seekho.

================================================================================
              3. AMBASSADOR RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

The Ambassador agrees to:

COMMUNITY BUILDING:
3.1  Build and manage the Seekho community at their college.
3.2  Recruit a minimum of ___ active users per month.
3.3  Organize at least ___ events/meetups per month (online or offline).
3.4  Onboard new users and help them understand the platform.
3.5  Foster an inclusive, welcoming environment for all students.

MODERATION:
3.6  Moderate the college community on the Seekho platform.
3.7  Enforce the Seekho Code of Conduct within their community.
3.8  Handle minor disputes and escalate serious issues to the core team
     within 24 hours.
3.9  Report any misuse, fake accounts, or violations promptly.

PROMOTION:
3.10 Promote Seekho through approved channels (social media, word of
     mouth, college events, notice boards, etc.).
3.11 Share content provided by Seekho on personal/college social media.
3.12 Represent Seekho positively and professionally at all times.

REPORTING:
3.13 Submit a monthly activity report to the Seekho core team.
3.14 Track community growth metrics (signups, active users, sessions).
3.15 Provide feedback and suggestions for platform improvement.
3.16 Attend monthly ambassador meetings/calls.

================================================================================
              4. AMBASSADOR SHALL NOT
================================================================================

The Ambassador agrees NOT to:

4.1  Collect any money or fees on behalf of Seekho without written
     authorization.
4.2  Make any commitments, promises, or guarantees on behalf of Seekho.
4.3  Modify, alter, or misuse the Seekho brand, logo, or materials.
4.4  Share confidential information about Seekho's business, strategy,
     code, or internal operations.
4.5  Use the Ambassador position for personal gain, political activities,
     or purposes unrelated to Seekho.
4.6  Engage in spamming, aggressive recruitment, or any practices that
     could harm Seekho's reputation.
4.7  Create unauthorized Seekho social media accounts or groups.
4.8  Organize events that involve alcohol, gambling, or any illegal
     activities.
4.9  Discriminate against or exclude any student from the community.
4.10 Simultaneously serve as an ambassador for a competing platform.

================================================================================
                   5. BRAND USAGE
================================================================================

5.1  The Ambassador may use Seekho branding materials ONLY as provided
     and approved by the Company.

5.2  All marketing materials, posters, and social media content must be
     approved by the Seekho team before distribution.

5.3  The Ambassador shall not create derivative logos, modify brand colors,
     or alter any official Seekho materials.

5.4  Upon termination, the Ambassador must immediately stop using all
     Seekho branding and return/delete any materials.

================================================================================
              7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
================================================================================

7.1  All content, materials, and strategies created by the Ambassador
     in their capacity as a Seekho Ambassador shall be the intellectual
     property of Seekho.

7.2  The Ambassador retains no rights to Seekho's intellectual property
     after termination.

7.3  Any ideas, suggestions, or improvements shared by the Ambassador
     regarding the platform become the property of Seekho.

7.4  The Ambassador shall not use knowledge gained during their tenure
     to create, support, or contribute to a competing platform.

================================================================================
              8. CONFIDENTIALITY
================================================================================

8.1  The Ambassador shall maintain strict confidentiality of all non-public
     information shared by Seekho, including but not limited to:
     - Business strategies and expansion plans
     - User data and analytics
     - Upcoming features and roadmaps
     - Internal communications and documents
     - Financial information

8.2  This confidentiality obligation survives termination of this
     Agreement for a period of TWO (2) years.

8.3  Breach of confidentiality may result in immediate termination and
     legal action.

================================================================================
              9. DATA PROTECTION
================================================================================

9.1  The Ambassador may have access to limited user data for moderation
     purposes. This data must NOT be:
     - Shared with any third party
     - Used for personal purposes
     - Stored on personal devices beyond what is necessary
     - Used for marketing or solicitation outside Seekho

9.2  The Ambassador shall comply with all applicable data protection laws.

================================================================================
              10. LIABILITY & INDEMNITY
================================================================================

10.1 The Ambassador shall conduct all activities safely and in compliance
     with college regulations and local laws.

10.2 Seekho shall not be liable for any injuries, damages, or losses
     incurred during Ambassador activities.

10.3 The Ambassador shall indemnify Seekho against any claims arising
     from the Ambassador's actions or negligence.

10.4 Seekho provides no insurance coverage for Ambassador activities.

================================================================================
              11. TERMINATION
================================================================================

This Agreement terminates:

11.1 At the end of the Term, unless renewed.
11.2 By either party with 15 days written notice.
11.3 Immediately by Seekho if the Ambassador:
     a) Violates this Agreement or the Code of Conduct
     b) Engages in misconduct or illegal activities
     c) Brings disrepute to Seekho
     d) Fails to meet minimum responsibilities for 2 consecutive months
     e) Is expelled or suspended from their college

Upon termination, the Ambassador must:
- Cease all activities on behalf of Seekho
- Return/delete all Seekho materials, data, and credentials
- Remove "Seekho Ambassador" from all profiles within 7 days
- Hand over community management to a designated successor

12.2 Disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in
     _______________, Maharashtra.
